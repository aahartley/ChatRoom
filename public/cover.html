Description

This project is a chat room implementation called Chat Room. The front page leads to a sign up/ log in page, which only asks for a username and password to avoid security issues. It also includes the link to this page, for our credits and bug reports. Once logged in the users, can see all registered users and whether or not they or online. The chat erases after 10 messages and their are emojis they can click or type in to use.
